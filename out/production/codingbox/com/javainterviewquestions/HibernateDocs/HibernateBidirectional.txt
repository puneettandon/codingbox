		Hibernate Bidirectional Mapping

Bidirectional association allows us to fetch details of dependent object from both side.
In such case, we have the reference of two classes in each other.

Let's take an example of Employee and Address, if Employee class has  a reference of Address and Address has a reference of Employee. 

Additionaly ,you have applied one to one or one to many relationship for the clasess in mapping file as well ,it is known as bidirectional association.
